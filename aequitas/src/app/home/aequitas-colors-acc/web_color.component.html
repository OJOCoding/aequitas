B.Contrast matrix

One of the main tools of palett.es is the contrast matrix. It visually shows us the levels of contrast between the colors that make up the palette. The lowest contrast level is 1:1 and the highest possible level is 21:1. The higher the contrast, the fewer problems viewing content/texts users will have. Color blind safe? If we read "YES" in this position, it indicates that this color combination is safe for the main types of color blindness. AAA/AA tells us whether this text/background color combination has enough contrast to meet the AAA or AA accessibility level at those sizes. Lastly the contrast index in numerical format.
