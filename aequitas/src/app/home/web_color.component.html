A. Colors Picked and why

Green is considered a good color due to its association with positive meanings such as nature, life, and well-being, as well as its proven ability to generate therapeutic and positive effects, fostering recovery and enhancing subjective well-being.

palett.es provides several indicators that will help you choose a color palette that is accessible to the greatest number of users following the WAGC2.0 guidelines in the contrast and color section.

B.Contrast matrix

One of the main tools of palett.es is the contrast matrix . It visually shows us the levels of contrast between the colors that make up the palette. The lowest contrast level is 1:1 and the highest possible level is 21:1 . The higher the contrast, the less problems viewing content/texts users will have. Color blind safe? If we read "YES" in this position, it indicates that this color combination is safe for the main types of color blindness. AAA/AA tells us whether this text/background color combination has enough contrast to meet the AAA or AA accessibility level at those sizes. Lastly the contrast index in numerical format.
